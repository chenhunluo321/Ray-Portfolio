Convenience

If I want to find a recipe for making a cake in the past. I have to spend a long time to go to the library, find the correct book, write down the recipe, and finally go back to home. All these procedures take me too much time which would discourage me from doing it. But now, I can open the search engine on my phone, and get all kinds of information immediately. I feel that internet and smartphone becomes an indispensable part of my life.
